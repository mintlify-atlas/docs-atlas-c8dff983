Victor Launcher is an in-game mod manager for Among Us built on BepInEx. Press **F7** while in-game to open the launcher, then install, toggle, or share DLL plugins — all without leaving the game or dragging files into folders manually.

## What Victor Launcher does

Victor Launcher removes the friction of manual mod management. Instead of dragging DLL files in and out of game folders, you control everything from an overlay that runs inside Among Us.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Victor Launcher">
    Place the Victor Launcher DLL in `BepInEx/plugins/` and launch Among Us.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the launcher in-game">
    Press **F7** at any time to show or hide the mod manager overlay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your mods">
    Click **Pick DLL** to select a mod file from your computer, or enter a room code to download a shared mod pack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and restart">
    Install queued mods from the launcher. When prompted, restart Among Us to load the new plugins.
  </Step>
</Steps>
